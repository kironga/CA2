About CA²

CA² (Authentication of Academic Credentials System) is a national digital platform for secure verification of academic qualifications using National ID and consent-based email OTP.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's trusted digital backbone for instant and credible academic credential verification across public and private sectors.

Our Mission

To reduce certificate fraud by connecting institutions, government, citizens, and employers through accurate records, secure OTP consent, and transparent verification workflows.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does CA² verification work?

HR enters National ID, the system sends an OTP to the citizen's registered email, and records are shown only after OTP validation.

Who can upload academic records?

Only authorized institution or exam body administrators can add or update records.

Can I update my OTP email?

Yes. Citizens can update profile details, including OTP email, from the Citizen dashboard.

Is verification access tracked?

Yes. Citizens can view businesses and HR users that have accessed their verified records.
